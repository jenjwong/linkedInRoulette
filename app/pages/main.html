LinkedIn Roulette allows Hack Reactor students to endorse each other en mass without the appearance of having done so. If your LinkedIn profile has the same few people endorsing your skills your profile loses credibility. LinkedIn Roulette makes it look like a more diverse pool of people have endorsed your skills. We will do LinkedIn Roulette face to face in a group setting, but it requires some set-up, so please follow the steps below:

1. Enter your GitHub handle and LinkedIn in the form.

2. Go through the list below and connect with classmates on LinkedIn. You will probably have to check back in a few days once everyone adds themselves to the list. This step is super important. Make sure you do it before the LinkedIn Roulette session!

3. Go to your LinkedIn profile and choose your top 9 skills for which you'd like to be endorsed. Put these at the top of your Skills and Endorsements List.

4. Wait for the LinkedIn Roulette session later this week to endorse people.
