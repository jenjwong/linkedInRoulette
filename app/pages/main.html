Linkedin Roulette
  • Play

LinkedIn Roulette allows Hack Reactor students to endorse each other en mass without the appearance of having done so. If your LinkedIn profile has the same few people endorsing your skills your profile looses credibility. LinkedIn Roulette makes it look like a more diverse pool of people have endorsed your skills.

1. Enter your GitHub handle and LinkedIn in the form below.

2. Go through the list below and connect with classmates on LinkedIn. You will probably have to check back in a few days to do this once everyone has added themselves.

3. Go to your LinkedIn profile and choose your top 9 skills for which you'd like to be endorsed. Put these at the top of your Skills and Endorsements List.

4. Wait for the LinkedIn Roulette session later this week to endorse people.
