Linkedin Roulette
  • Play

1. Enter your GitHub handle and LinkedIn in the form below

2. Connect with classmates on LinkedIn. You will probably have to check back in a few days to do this once everyone has added themselves

3. Go to your LinkedIn profile and choose your top 12 skills for which you'd like to be endorsed. Put these at the top of your Skills and Endorsements List

4. Wait for the LinkedIn Roulette session later this week to endorse people. LinkedIn Roulette is designed so that Hack Reactor students can endorse each other en mass, without the appearance of having done so. If your LinkedIn profile has the same few people endorsing your for skills your profile looses credibility. LinkedIn Roulette makes it look like a more diverse pool of people have endorsed your skill sets.
